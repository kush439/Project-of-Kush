History Lalji Mehrotra (1900-1988) LML School is one of the pioneer projects of Lions club of Karnavati Foundation. The foundation is affiliated to the Lions Club International, an organization with presence in over 189 countries and a name synonymous with service to society, sacrifice and voluntarism. The Lions Club of Karnavati is one of the largest, most active and well-managed clubs in the world. The foundation aims to serve society by fulfilling two fundamental needs – Health and Education. In keeping with this quest, the foundation has set-up a school and an eye hospital. Lalji Mehrotra Lions School is set-up adjacent to the Lions Karnavati Vishnubhai Patel Eye Hospital at Ognaj, about 10 Kms. from Thaltej Tekra, Ahmedabad. The school began operations in June 2002 with classes Jr. KG to 4, adding one class every year and is now running classes from Jr.KG to Twelve. The school is affiliated to the CISCE [Council for The Indian School Certificate Examinations], New Delhi. Philosophy LML School provides each student with an environment where learning is defined by the child’s inherent desire to learn, inspired by a philosophy of Love, Joy and Faith in the child as a learner, and guided by education research and knowledge development. The school promotes the development of each child intellectually, emotionally, physically and spiritually and is committed to helping them learn to know themselves and to enhance their choices later on in life. Values LML School is a co-educational institution whose students will stand for and strive to uphold the following values and qualities: Intellectual Competence Sensitivity Dignity of labour Responsibility Excellence Positive Attitude Community Servic

Achivement

Our students are encouraged to become multi-faceted personalities by not just excelling in academics but by taking part in all kinds of competitions at the inter-school and inter-district level. Training for it starts early as students compete in various inter-house events. 100% result in the ICSE and ISC Examinations every year. Excellence in sports like Volleyball, Football, Cricket and Athletics (Boys and girls teams). Awards and recognition for Debating, Poetry Writing and Recitation and Declamation at Inter School, Inter District and State level. Awards and recognition in creative disciplines like Art, Craft, Music and Dance at Inter-School, Regional and National level. Students have been selected to play "Volleyball & Football" nationals. Girls Volleyball team is the Winner of the NWR in the Nationals.

Our Mission & Goal

Mission The academic mission of LML School is to enable students from Kindergarten through grade Twelve, to strive for excellence and foster a spirit of inquiry for lifelong learning in a changing world. The school endeavours to prepare young children for a life of productivity and service in a global community by promoting the confidence and courage needed to assume a leadership role. The school challenges each student to develop personal integrity, respect for the dignity and a sense of responsibility for their actions. Goal It is our goal to help each child blossom into a harmonious and balanced personality with a deep sense of concern for service to society, family values and patriotism.
